Help Center

Frequently Asked Questions

For Pet Owners

Q: How do I find a service provider?

A: Browse our homepage to see available services. We are currently in the onboarding phase, and more providers will be listed soon.

Q: Are service providers verified?

A: Yes, all service providers go through our verification process to ensure quality and reliability.

Q: How do I book a service?

A: Once we complete onboarding, you'll be able to browse providers and book services directly through our platform.

For Service Providers

Q: How do I join as a service provider?

A: Visit our Join page and fill out the onboarding form with your business details.

Q: What services can I offer?

A: We welcome providers offering grooming, training, walking, boarding, and other pet-related services.

Q: Is there a fee to join?

A: Please contact us for information about our pricing structure and commission rates.

General Questions

Q: What areas do you serve?

A: We are expanding our service areas. Please check back regularly or contact us to inquire about your specific location.

Q: How can I contact support?

A: Visit our Contact page to send us a message or email us at support@pettomets.com.

Need More Help?

If you can't find the answer to your question, please don't hesitate to contact us. We're here to help!
